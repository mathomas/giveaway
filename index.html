Instructions

Welcome to the Pentax ME Giveaway! In order to be entered into the drawing for the camera kit, you need to carefully follow the 5 easy steps below.

When the time limit is up, I'll put all the valid entries into a drawing to determine the winner. I will contact the winner by email to get the mailing address.

If the instructions aren't followed you will not be entered in the drawing!

  1. Subscribe to my YouTube channel. Only subscribers can win.

  2. Find the 5 "quest keys" in my photography videos.

    A quest key will look something like this: quest key: snarfle, and will pop up in the video for a few seconds.

    The quest keys will appear within the first couple of minutes of a number of my videos, so you'll have to do some hunting.

  3. Come back here and enter the quest keys (one at a time) into the form above, and click the "Get Quest" button to have each quest revealed.

    I suggest you try typing snarfle in the form now, just for fun!

  4. Perform the quests (all 5 of them), and take notes! You'll have to do some detective work, but everything you need to know in order to perform the quests is in my YouTube videos, or their descriptions, etc.

    You have to perform all the quests, and get the answers/findings correct to win.

  5. Once your quests are complete, send an email to pentaxme@me.com with the following:
    • Your youtube username (so I can check that you are subscribed)
    • Your quest findings. All the quests must be completed. Give the quest key and your findings.

    You might want to check out the example email below. Really closely.
